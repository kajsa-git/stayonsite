## Key Insights You'll Find

### Across All Documents

**The Problem:**
Your website feels corporate/impersonal despite founder excellence being your actual advantage.

**The Solution:**
Make founder presence a TRUST SIGNAL (direct access = no bureaucracy) instead of an apology ("we're just two people").

**The Psychology:**
Construction PMs are burned by corporate hand-offs. Seeing founder names + direct phone/SMS contact = relief signal.

**The Implementation:**
Three tiers: Footer (30 min) → Services (20 min) → Optional photos (30 min)

**The Differentiation:**
"Fast response" = any company. "Direct from Kajsa or Natalie" = competitive advantage.

## Making Your Decision

### You should implement this if:
- Kajsa & Natalie want founder visibility
- You can maintain 24h response times
- You want to differentiate from larger competitors
- You're willing to have founder names in customer-facing materials

### You should NOT implement if:
- Response times can't match messaging (48h is too slow for "direct from founder")
- Founders uncomfortable with visibility
- You need to hide accountability
- Tone doesn't match rest of brand
