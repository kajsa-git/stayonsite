## The Core Problem & Solution

### Problem

### Solution

## The Psychology: Why This Works

**Your customer is a stressed construction PM who:**
- Has been burned by corporate bureaucracy ("transferred 3 times")
- Needs one person who answers ("I don't know who I'm talking to")
- Values decisiveness ("stop asking for approval")

**What kills deals:**
- "We'll check with my manager"
- 2-3 day response times
- Different person each time
- "Standard process" language

**What wins deals:**
- "I'm checking now and calling you in 30 minutes"
- Same person follows through
- Founder names = personal accountability
- Decision-maker visible, not hidden

**Why founder names work:**
- Not "small" (you have 10+ years, 2000+ placements)
- "Owned" (founders actually make decisions)
- "Transparent" (no mystery about who's in charge)

## FAQ

**Q: Won't this make us look small?**
A: No. You have 10+ years, 2000+ placements, 200+ testimonials. Founder names signal "owned and accountable," not "just two people." The difference is framing.

**Q: What if we can't respond in 24 hours?**
A: Don't emphasize speed + founder presence together. Use founder names for accountability ("you reach the decision-maker"), not speed promises.

**Q: Should we use titles (Founder, CEO)?**
A: No. Just first names (Kajsa & Natalie). Titles make it marketing-y; first names feel more direct.

**Q: What if one founder leaves?**
A: Keep both names while both are active. If extended absence, switch to "our founding team" temporarily.

**Q: Do we need professional photos?**
A: No. Tier 1 & 2 work without them. Founder cards (Tier 3) only worth doing if photos are professional-grade.
