## Visual Flow: Buyer Journey

### Scenario: Construction PM Visits Site

```
STEP 1: Lands on Homepage
├─ Hero section (no founder visibility yet)
│
STEP 2: Scrolls to Services Section
├─ Sees "Kajsa eller Natalie läser förfrågan direkt" (Step 1)
├─ Sees "Kajsa eller Natalie ringer dig" (Step 2) ← FOUNDER NAME REGISTERS
├─ Sees "Samma person som svarade" (Step 3) ← ACCOUNTABILITY SIGNAL
├─ Reads narrative: "Kajsa och Natalie äger dessa processer helt" ← CONTEXT
│
STEP 3: Reads References
├─ Sees customer testimonials
├─ Bottom of page: "Med omsorg från StayOnSite. Kajsa & Natalie" ← REINFORCEMENT
│
STEP 4: Scrolls to References Section (if available)
├─ Sees "Kajsa & Natalie" founder cards ← HUMANIZATION (optional)
│
STEP 5: Fills Out Form
├─ Sees founder signature in form submission email ← CLOSING SIGNAL
```

**Psychological effect:** By the time PM receives confirmation email, they've seen "Kajsa and Natalie" mentioned 6-7 times in context of speed/accountability. Founder names now = speed/reliability in their mind.

## Brand Consistency Check

### Messaging Should Feel Consistent With:

```
CURRENT NORDIC/MINIMAL BRAND
├─ Light typography (Montserrat, Inter)
├─ Plenty of whitespace
├─ Professional tone
├─ Concrete language (not fluffy)
├─ Customer-focused (not company self-promotion)
└─ Direct, clear CTAs

FOUNDER MESSAGING FITS IF:
├─ Names appear without fanfare ("Kajsa or Natalie", not "Our Brilliant Co-Founder")
├─ Used functionally ("who you reach", not "meet the team")
├─ Lowercase context ("kajsa or natalie handles your request" not "KAJSA & NATALIE")
├─ Concrete benefit ("no queue" not "personal attention")
├─ Professional photos (if used) maintain Nordic minimalism
└─ Quote is straightforward, not quirky
```

- [ ] No layout breaks from added text

---

## Key Takeaway

Founder names should appear **naturally in context of speed/accountability**, not as a design element.

They should feel like functional information ("who you reach") not decorative marketing ("meet our amazing founders").

Visitors should see the names multiple times and connect them to the fast response promise. By the time they submit, founder names = speed = trust.

That's the visual strategy.
