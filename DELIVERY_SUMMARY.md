## Red Flags to Watch For

### This Could Go Wrong If:
- Response times slip below 24h (messaging becomes lie)
- Only one founder visible (fairness issue)
- Tone becomes casual (breaks professional brand)
- You're not actually available (customers realize it's BS)

### Prevention:
- Commit to response times BEFORE deploying
- Both names appear equally
- Keep professional, warm tone
- Be genuinely available for calls/emails
