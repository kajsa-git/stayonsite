## Tone Guidelines

- Professional + warm
- Concrete (Kajsa or Natalie, not "our team")

### AVOID:

## Quick Troubleshooting

**Q: Won't this make us look small?**
A: No. Founder direct access isn't small—it's leveraged ownership. You have 10+ years, 2000+ placements, 200+ testimonials. You're established. Founder names just attach accountability to that track record.

**Q: What if one founder is unavailable?**
A: Still use both names (both own the business). If extended absence, mention "founding team" temporarily. Don't hide it.

**Q: Should we add titles (CEO, Founder)?**
A: No. Just first names. Let the context imply leadership. "Kajsa & Natalie" is stronger than "Kajsa & Natalie, Co-Founders."

**Q: What if response times slip?**
A: This is critical. You MUST maintain 24h proposals, 15min calls. If you can't, don't use founder names with speed claims. Use: "Your contact is directly Kajsa or Natalie" (access, not speed promise).

**Q: Do we need professional photos?**
A: Only for Tier 3 (founder cards). Tier 1 & 2 work without them. If photos exist and are professional, add them. If you'd need new photos taken, it's not essential.
